# agent-continuity-kit

## Stage

`sprout`

## What This Line Is

A small public repo that turns a private continuity practice into a copyable
structure for long-running AI-assisted projects.

## Current Shape

The repo should show `AGENTS.md`, `continuity/MAP.md`, and
`continuity/lines/` as the minimal continuity surface.
It must not become a full personal operating system.

## What Changed Recently

Older continuity attempts were recognized as valuable but too heavy. The new
structure is ethos -> map -> living lines.

## Drift Risks

Overexplaining, adding too many docs, turning the kit into a framework, or
selling it as magical AI memory.

## Next Useful Move

Create the smallest public version that demonstrates the gesture without
importing private project context.
